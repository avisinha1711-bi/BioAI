How It Works

  • Enter a DNA sequence in the 3' to 5' direction
  • The system generates the complementary DNA strand
  • Transcription creates mRNA (A→U, T→A, G→C, C→G)
  • Translation converts mRNA codons to amino acids
  • Stop codons (UAA, UAG, UGA) terminate translation

Genetic Code

  • Adenine (A) pairs with Thymine (T) in DNA
  • Guanine (G) pairs with Cytosine (C) in DNA
  • In RNA, Thymine (T) is replaced with Uracil (U)
  • AUG is the start codon (Methionine)
  • UAA, UAG, UGA are stop codons
